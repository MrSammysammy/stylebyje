About Stylebyjc

Designing Bridal Gowns That Tell Your Story

Stylebyjc is a bridal fashion brand dedicated to creating timeless, sophisticated pieces for the modern bride. Every gown is designed to reflect individuality, elegance, and unforgettable beauty.

Our Story

Where Craftsmanship Meets Bridal Elegance

At Stylebyjc, we believe every bride deserves to feel seen, celebrated, and unforgettable on her special day.

Our journey is rooted in a deep passion for craftsmanship and a love for bringing bridal dreams to life. Each gown is thoughtfully designed and carefully handcrafted, with attention given to every detail — from fabric selection to the final finishing.

We work closely with every bride, ensuring that each piece reflects her unique personality, style, and vision.

  • Bespoke bridal design experience
  • Luxury fabrics and detailing
  • Personalized design process
  • Timeless, elegant silhouettes

Founder

Olajumoke Olasupo

Stylebyjc was founded by Olajumoke Olasupo, a passionate fashion designer with a deep love for craftsmanship and detail.

Her journey into fashion began with a natural passion for creating — an ability to transform fabrics into something truly remarkable.

Her transition into bridal design was inspired by her desire to be part of one of the most important moments in a woman’s life — her wedding day.

Through Stylebyjc, she has created a space where brides are heard, understood, and beautifully expressed through design.
